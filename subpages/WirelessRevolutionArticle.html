wireLESS revolution

By Kristine Dzumakajeva

Even if you use or not, everyone knows what is headphones. There are so many variates of headphones nowadays that everyone can find the best option suiting their needs. Design, features, quality and price - are usually the main criteria of choosing headphones.

Nowadays is hard for some people to imagine their life without headphones, especially if you are using them daily. 100 years ago headphones had totally different mission on earth and they were hardly connected to music at all..

Wireless headphones

Although the first consumer bluetooth headphones were invented on 1999, it took many more years to adapt them for audio streaming capabilities and improve the sound quality. Anyway, this is the reason mainstream headphones are now going wireless.

Sound quality

Back in time a lot of people preferred wired headphones instead of wireless because of the lack of sound quality. But technologies since then went far and now wireless headphones can really compete. Try Bose Noise Cancelling Headphones 700 and you will experience an immersive listening experience at any volume with signature active EQ and impressive bass response.

Battery

Yes, maybe you thought that this is one of the main disadvantage of wireless headphones. And maybe it was years ago. But we are working on improvements and now there are much longer battery lives than before. Also some of the headphones has a charging case, and putting there just for a 15 minutes charge will give you a 45 minutes of battery for your headphones! SoundSport Free wireless headphone charging case will keep your headphones safe and will provide you two additional charges!

Additional features

Modern headphones has playback buttons. This button allows you to play, pause, skip and adjust volume, decline or answer a phone call. Some headphones also has an ability to measure your data like - heart rate and your moving speed. This data can be valuable for athletes or people trying to get in shape.

Bose SoundSport Pulse Wireless Headphones have a built-in heart rate monitor. They connect to your smartphone with an app. This can allow you to track and be coached your training. Combined with waterproof material, these headphones are perfect for anyone who is into sports.
